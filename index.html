About Us

Suruchi Foods, established in 2002 in India, is dedicated to providing high-quality, hygienic, and delicious food. Through strategic partnerships and commitment to excellence, it has expanded to 60 malls and 17 towns by 2018, employing over 600 individuals. The company's passion is to bring joy and satisfaction to customers, making it a trusted name in culinary delight.
